Making waffles is not hard - just try!

Description

Waffles - what's there to say? They taste so good and you can add all of your favorite toppings. So let's make them!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • flour
  • milk
  • oil
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • vanilla
  • your favorite waffle toppings (eg. maple syrup, fresh berries, chocolate sauce, whipped cream)

Steps

  1. Whisk two eggs
  2. Add flour
  3. Add milk and oil
  4. Whisk in the sugar
  5. Stir in the rest of the ingredients
  6. Pour the mixture onto the preheated waffle iron
  7. Cook until golden brown
  8. Add your favorite toppings and enjoy!
