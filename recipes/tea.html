The making of tea - how hard could it be

Description

Tea - one of many things that the British gave to the world. of course, they had stolen it beforehand. This fabulous recipe will guide you through the process of making this drink.

Ingredients

  • tea leafs or a teabag
  • water
  • sugar, lemon, honey, milk (additional, according to personal taste)

Steps

  1. Insert the teabag or add your tea leafs into a cup
  2. Boil some water in a kettle
  3. Pour the boiling water into the cup
  4. Leave the tea for 3-5 minutes
  5. Remove the teabag
  6. Add your favorite ingredients and stir the tea
  7. Enjoy! Be careful - the tea is hot!
